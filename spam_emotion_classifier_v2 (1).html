⚙️ How This Project Works
🔢
TF-IDF Vectorizer
Converts raw email text into numerical feature vectors. Terms frequent in one email but rare across the dataset get higher weights — perfect for spotting spam keywords.
🤖
Naive Bayes Classifier
MultinomialNB trained on 1,500 labeled emails. Uses Bayes' theorem to calculate spam probability. Fast, lightweight, and highly effective for text classification.
💬
TextBlob Sentiment
Computes polarity (−1 to +1) and subjectivity (0 to 1). Combined with rule-based keyword scoring for a hybrid emotion detection engine.
🎭
5-Class Emotion Engine
Classifies legitimate emails into Urgent 🚨, Angry 😡, Happy 😊, Sad 😢, or Neutral 😐 using keyword scores + polarity + subjectivity signals.
